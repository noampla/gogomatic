Game Overview

Gogomatics is a chaotic, cursed card game where players must survive magical limitations, sabotage opponents, and attempt to win by either outlasting everyone or gathering four special blessings. Each player starts with a curse, forcing them to follow absurd rules. Throughout the game, players draw from a deck containing Curses, Blessings, and Special Cards, using strategy and timing to eliminate rivals or secure victory.

Game Setup

Choosing Curses

  • Before the game starts, players can either choose which curses are in play or randomly draw one from the full list.
  • There must be the same number of curses in play as there are players.
  • Each player randomly draws a Curse Card from the Curses Deck and places it face-up in front of them.

Important: Any Curse Cards not chosen for the game are removed from the Main Game Deck.

Deck Preparation

  • Remove from the Main Game Deck any Curse Cards that were not chosen for the game.
  • Shuffle the remaining deck.

Starting Hand

  • Each player is dealt 3 cards from the Main Game Deck.
  • If a player receives a Special Card, they must place one face-up in their Special Card Slot.
  • A player can only hold 1 Special Card at a timeâ€”if they receive more than one, they must choose one to keep and discard the rest to the Trash Deck.

Deciding First Player

Suggested method: Each player draws a card from the deckâ€”whoever gets the highest number or a â€œmagicalâ€ card (like a Blessing) goes first. Turns proceed clockwise.

Decks in Play

Three central decks are placed in the middle:

  • Take Deck (Face Down): The main draw pile.
  • Open Deck (Face Up): Cards drawn from the Take Deck can be placed here.
  • Trash Deck (Face Down): Used for discarded cards.

Turn Structure

  1. Drawing a Card: Players must take a card from either the Open Deck (the face-up card) or the Take Deck (flip a new card into the Open Deck, then choose whether to take it or leave it). Special Rule for Curses: If a player flips their own Curse Card, they immediately discard it into the Trash Deck.
  2. Managing Cards: - If the player takes a Special Card, they must place it in their Special Slot (discarding any previous Special Card). - Players cannot hold Special Cards in their hand. - If a player exceeds 5 cards in hand, they must discard a card before drawing.
  3. Performing an Action (Choose 1): - Use a Special Card (from the Special Card Slot). - Place a Curse on a Player (only if that player has The Book of Spells on them).

Elimination Rules

How a Player Gets Eliminated

  • Book of Spells Activation: If a player has The Book of Spells in their Curse Pile, their Curse is â€œactivated,â€ allowing other players to add Curse Cards to their pile.
  • Reaching 3 Curse Cards: If a player accumulates 3 copies of their own Curse Card (including the original), they are eliminated. The Spell of Spells (Joker) can be used as the final Curse to complete an elimination.
  • Breaking Their Curse Rule: If a player fails to follow their Curse's restriction, the game pauses. Any player holding that Curse Card must place it on their Curse Pile. If no one has the card, it is fetched from the deck and placed on their pile. The deck is reshuffled, and the game resumes.

What Happens When a Player is Eliminated?

The eliminated playerâ€™s Curse Cards are removed from the game. Their remaining cards are shuffled back into the central decks. The next turn goes to the player after the last one who played.

Winning the Game

A player wins in one of two ways:

  1. Collecting 4 Blessing Cards: If a player gathers 4 Blessings, they win immediately. The Spell of Spells (Joker) can replace a missing Blessing if activated in the Special Card Slot.
  2. Last Player Standing: If all other players are eliminated, the last survivor wins.

Card Types & Explanations

Gogomatic uses three main categories of cards:

  • Curse Cards: The twisted limitations each player must follow.
  • Blessing Cards: Collect 4 to win instantly.
  • Special Cards: Game-changers that influence curses and blessings.

List of Curses

Special Cards (×§×œ×¤×™ ×§×¡×)

Special Cards introduce unique effects that can change the course of the game. Players can only activate these from their Special Card Slot, not directly from their hand.

  • The Book of Spells (Ace): Activates a player's curse and makes them vulnerable to elimination. Once active, other players can place additional Curse Cards on them.
  • The Spellbreaker: Cancels and removes The Book of Spells. If a player has The Spellbreaker, they are no longer affected by their curse and cannot receive additional Curse Cards.
  • The Spell of Spells (Joker): A powerful wild card that can be used to complete a set of 4 Blessings or as the final Curse needed to eliminate a player.
  • Fortune Draw: The player draws 2 extra cards from the deck.
