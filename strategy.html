Gogomatic Codex

Master the chaos. Outplay your rivals. Become the last one standing.

🔥 Beginner Tips

Every player starts cursed—embrace it. Don't rush your moves. Pay attention to other players' curses and force them into mistakes.

⚔️ Advanced Tactics

Use The Book of Spells to activate opponents' curses, but don’t expose yourself too early. Special Cards are game changers—time them wisely.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • Forgetting your own curse—watch your behavior!
  • Holding too many cards—keep your deck lean.
  • Underestimating Blessings—4 of them win the game instantly.

🏆 How to Win

Two paths: Eliminate rivals or collect 4 Blessings. Decide your playstyle early. If others are aggressive, play defensive and seek blessings.
