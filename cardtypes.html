The Gogomatic Collection

Discover the cursed, blessed, and magical cards that shape your fate.

Curses

Each player receives one Curse at the start—limit your moves, warp your speech, or bind your hands. Disobey and the deck may claim you.

Announcer Curse

Must announce when any action is taken (e.g. playing a Special Card).

Apology Curse

Must say "sorry" whenever a player is eliminated.

Compliment Curse

Must compliment someone whenever a player draws a card.

Gogomatic Curse

Must say "Gogomatic" before drawing a card.

Weak Hand Curse

Use only your non-dominant hand.

Avoid-Name Curse

Cannot say another player's name.

Royal Curse

Refer to yourself with a royal title (e.g., "His Majesty").

Salute Curse

Must salute whenever a player is eliminated.

Silence Curse

You cannot speak at all.

Third-Person Curse

Must speak in the third person at all times.

Card Whisperer Curse

Must whisper for the entire game.

Blessings

Gather four to win instantly. Each Blessing grants a glimmer of hope in a realm of curses.

Luck Blessing

Fortune aligns your draw.

Strength Blessing

Empowers your hand with mighty force.

Protection Blessing

Shields you from impending doom.

Wisdom Blessing

Enhances your insight and strategy.

Time Blessing

Manipulates time in your favor.

Phoenix Blessing

Ignites rebirth from ashes.

Special Cards

Game-changers that can seal an opponent’s fate or free you from your own doom.

Book of Spells

Mark a foe’s Curse for activation.

Spellbreaker

Nullify the Book of Spells with swift magic.

Spell of Spells

A wild card—complete Blessings or finish a Curse set.

Fortune Draw

Draw 2 extra cards at a crucial moment.
