The Digital Grimoire

Enter this holographic spellbook to learn the arcane rules that bind the game.

Remove all Special Cards. Each player receives one visible curse card. Remove duplicates of each player’s curse from the deck. Deal 5 cards to each player (or fewer if the deck is low).

On your turn, you must take a card from either the face‑up pile or the deck. If you don’t, you must play a Special Card (if available). Don’t exceed 5 cards in hand; discard extra cards face‑down.

Breaking your curse or drawing 3 extra copies of it from the deck results in elimination. Beware the cursed fate!

Win by being the last player alive or by collecting four Blessing cards for an instant victory.
